How it works

Built for parents who want the sensible option to be the easy one.

Sapling is the middleman between parents, donors and Junior ISA providers. Parents create a child's fund, choose a provider, then share a link or QR code so family and friends can contribute directly into their future savings.

1

Create the fund

Add your child, choose a provider and get a ready-to-share savings page in minutes.

2

Share it anywhere

Birthdays, Christmas, baby showers, christenings, school events or the family WhatsApp chat.

3

Watch it grow

See contributions, projected future value and the impact of family support all in one place.
