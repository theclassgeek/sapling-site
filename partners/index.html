FAQs

The practical questions a good partner will ask early.

Do I need a huge audience?

No. Sapling is particularly interested in trusted small operators with the right audience, not just the biggest list.

Will the full revenue model be public?

No. The page explains the commercial structure clearly, but fuller economics are reserved for approved partners.

Who is the programme designed for first?

Prenatal classes, nurseries, individual teachers, and other small family-facing businesses with real parent trust.

Why not lead with bigger institutions?

Because Sapling is more likely to convert well through trusted local recommendations than through slow, institutional partnership cycles.
